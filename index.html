Beginner Fat Loss Workout Plan

Monday: Upper Body

Bench Press - 4 sets × 10 reps
Shoulder Press - 4 sets × 12 reps
Lat Pulldown - 4 sets × 12 reps
Dumbbell Rows - 3 sets × 12 reps
Bicep Curls - 3 sets × 15 reps
Triceps Pushdown - 3 sets × 15 reps
Cardio: 15-20 minutes treadmill incline walk

Tuesday: Legs

Squats - 4 sets × 12 reps
Romanian Deadlifts - 4 sets × 12 reps
Leg Press - 4 sets × 12 reps
Calf Raises - 4 sets × 15 reps
Lunges - 3 sets × 12 reps
Cardio: 10 minutes

Wednesday: Abs & Cardio

30-40 minutes walking or cycling
Planks - 3 sets × 60 seconds
Leg Raises - 3 sets × 15 reps
Russian Twists - 3 sets × 20 reps

Thursday: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Incline Dumbbell Press - 4 sets × 10 reps
Shoulder Raises - 3 sets × 12 reps
Triceps Rope Pushdown - 3 sets × 15 reps
Shoulder Dumbbell Press - 3 sets × 12 reps
Close-Grip Push-Ups - 3 sets × 15 reps
Cardio: 15-20 minutes Stairmaster

Friday: Back & Biceps

Deadlifts - 4 sets × 10 reps
Lat Pulldown - 4 sets × 12 reps
Barbell Rows - 4 sets × 12 reps
Face Pulls - 3 sets × 12 reps
Hammer Curls - 3 sets × 12 reps
Cardio: 10 minutes rowing machine

Saturday & Sunday:

Light Cardio (optional)
Rest or active recovery
